Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis of
E-Commerce Reviews

Millions of Shopee reviews are posted every day, but a simple star rating doesn't tell sellers what customers actually liked or disliked. This system analyzes customer reviews based on different aspects—Product Quality, Packaging Quality, and Delivery & Service— to help sellers identify strengths, understand customer concerns, and improve their products and services.

Product Quality

Tracks feedback on quality, durability, and whether the item is genuine and worth its price.

Packaging Quality

Flags mentions of bubble wrap, boxes, and seals to spot products that arrived broken or damaged.

Delivery & Service

Tracks delivery and service efficiency: shipping delays and how sellers respond to their customers.
